RUTTER KYRIE ELEISON (from Requiem)
Chord Chart and Recording Guide for GarageBand

COMPOSER: John Rutter (contemporary, 1975)
KEY: A major
TEMPO: ~72 BPM (gently flowing)
TIME: 4/4

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

LYRICS (Latin):

Kyrie eleison. Kyrie eleison. Kyrie eleison.
Christe eleison. Christe eleison. Christe eleison.
Kyrie eleison. Kyrie eleison. Kyrie eleison.
Amen.

[Translation: Lord, have mercy. Christ, have mercy. Lord, have mercy. Amen.]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CHORD PROGRESSION (main section):

| A     | F#m    | D      | E      |
Ky  -  rie    e  -  lei  -  son.    (repeat)

| A     | F#m    | D      | A      |
(soft resolution back to home)

SECONDARY SECTION (Christe):

| D     | Bm     | G      | A      |
Chris  -  te    e  -  lei  -  son.

| D     | Bm     | E      | A      |
(returns to A major)

STRUCTURE:

Intro (8 measures): Fingerpicking on A major, establish the mood
Verse 1 (16 measures): "Kyrie eleison" x3 — gentle, contemplative
Verse 2 (16 measures): "Christe eleison" x3 — slightly more animated
Verse 3 (16 measures): "Kyrie eleison" x3 — return to opening energy

GARAGEBAND RECORDING TIPS:

1. INSTRUMENTS:
   - Acoustic guitar (warm nylon or folk setting)
   - Vocal (solo voice, intimate, no strain)
   - Optional: add a single string pad (Strings → Warm Pad) at 20% volume for texture

2. RECORDING APPROACH:
   - This piece is SHORT—about 3-4 minutes total
   - Record guitar first as a reference
   - Vocal should be laid back and contemplative
   - Use natural phrasing—"Kyrie" is the plea, "eleison" is the response

3. MICROPHONE/VOCAL:
   - Sing at a comfortable volume (medium, not loud)
   - This is prayer music—restraint is beauty
   - Distance from mic: 6-8 inches for intimacy without plosives
   - No compression; let the voice breathe naturally

4. MIXING:
   - Vocal sits slightly above guitar (maybe 2-3 dB louder)
   - Light reverb on vocal only (Room, 12-15%)
   - Guitar: dry or subtle reverb (8-10%)
   - Master: keep it simple, no limiting

5. EXPORT:
   - AAC 256 kbps or .wav
   - File name: "Rutter_Kyrie_Eleison_Steve_Price_[DATE]"

HARMONIC NOTES:

Rutter's Kyrie uses major key harmonic language (A major) which is unusual 
for this text—traditionally Kyrie is somber (minor). Rutter's choice creates
a sense of hope and gentleness rather than darkness. This modern sensibility
is perfect for a meditative/devotional setting.

The key modulation from A to D (Christe section) creates intimacy—a shift 
inward before returning to the original key.
