LYRICS (King James English):

The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want.
He maketh me to lie down in green pastures:
He leadeth me beside the still waters.
He restoreth my soul:
He leadeth me in the paths of righteousness for his name's sake.
Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death,
I will fear no evil: for thou art with me;
Thy rod and thy staff they comfort me.
Thou preparest a table before me in the presence of mine enemies:
Thou anointest my head with oil; my cup runneth over.
Surely goodness and mercy shall follow me all the days of my life:
And I will dwell in the house of the Lord forever.

GARAGEBAND RECORDING TIPS:

1. VOCAL APPROACH:
   - This is the MOST COMFORTING scripture passage in the Bible
   - Sing with warmth, assurance, and peace—not melancholy
   - The valley section (yea, though I walk...) is brief; then reassurance returns
   - Think: shepherd's care, not darkness

2. GUITAR STYLE:
   - Pastoral, flowing fingerpicking
   - Use open strings (let them ring naturally)
   - This should sound like folk music—not too refined
   - Warm nylon strings work beautifully here

3. MICROPHONE SETUP:
   - Vocal: 8 inches (warm, forward position)
   - Guitar: 2-3 feet (capture open string resonance)
   - Record in a space with light natural reverb (living room is ideal)

4. RECORDING SEQUENCE:
   - Guitar first (2 takes; this is long, keep the most consistent)
   - Vocal: 2-3 full takes
   - Comp the best sections
   - This is quite long; allow natural variation in delivery

5. MIXING:
   - Vocal: slightly prominent (2-3 dB above guitar)
   - Vocal reverb: Room, 12-15%
   - Guitar: minimal reverb (8%) or dry (let open strings naturally bloom)
   - Master: warm, open, peaceful quality
   - NO compression (dynamic range preserves the intimacy)

6. EXPORT:
   - AAC 256 kbps or .wav
   - File name: "Psalm_23_Lord_is_My_Shepherd_Steve_Price_[DATE]"

SPIRITUAL CONTEXT:

Psalm 23 is recited at:
- Funerals (comfort in grief)
- Healing services (restoration theme)
- Daily devotions (assurance in uncertainty)
- Children's bedtime prayers (trust, protection)

Your recording should convey:
- Peace in the Lord's care (main theme)
- Confidence even in difficulty (valley section)
- Gratitude and abundance (table section)
- Eternal hope (conclusion)

HISTORICAL NOTES:

Psalm 23 is the most beloved psalm worldwide. It's been set to music for centuries:
- Traditional hymn tune (what we use here)
- Dvorak, Charles Gounod, various composers
- Modern praise songs

Your simple guitar + voice version honors the original's intimate spirituality.
